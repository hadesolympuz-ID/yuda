# Why YUDA Exists

Knowledge changes.

Understanding evolves.

Ideas collapse.

Some ideas survive.

Others return years later in a different form.

Most systems preserve only the latest version.

YUDA preserves the journey.

Every revision matters.

Every contradiction matters.

Every abandoned idea matters.

Because today's discarded idea may become tomorrow's missing piece.

YUDA does not preserve certainty.

YUDA preserves the evolution of understanding.
