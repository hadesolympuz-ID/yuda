# Philosophy

YUDA is built upon a simple belief:

Understanding is not something we possess.

It is something we continuously build.

The purpose of YUDA is not to preserve answers,
but to preserve the journey that leads to those answers.

Every log is a snapshot of understanding at a particular moment.

As our understanding evolves,
new logs should be created instead of rewriting the past.

Knowledge is never complete.

Understanding never ends.
